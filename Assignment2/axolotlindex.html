THE AXOLOTL

  • Hey, it's me, an Axolotl!

  • Axolotl's are my favorite animal for a lot of reasons. Maybe a big reason is its uncanny resemblance to a favorite childhood Pokemon, but I digress.

    Aside from looking really cool, the Axolotl is a rare animal in that it develops neotenously. That is, it doesn't mature until specific external conditions are met. In other words, it's an immortal man-child until it comes into contact with iodine.

  • As of 2013 I'm pretty much extint!
